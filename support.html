Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account at any time by going to Settings → Profile → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.
How do subscriptions work?
REALones offers three tiers:
  • Free: Up to 32 friends
  • Social ($4.99/month): Up to 100 friends
  • Inner Circle ($9.99/month): Up to 200 friends
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID or Google Play account and will auto-renew unless canceled 24 hours before the next billing period.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
  • iOS: Settings app → Your Name → Subscriptions → REALones → Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → REALones → Cancel
Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
How do I block or report someone?
To block or report a user, tap the three dots (...) on their post or profile and select "Block User" or "Report." Blocked users cannot see your content or contact you.
How does the Pioneer system work?
New users need to earn 5 credits to unlock full access to REALones. Earn credits by:
  • Inviting friends who join (1 credit per friend)
  • Importing Facebook friends (1 credit)
  • Completing your profile (1 credit)
  • Importing phone contacts (1 credit)
What is the Memory Book?
The Memory Book lets you preserve important moments with your friends, even from before you joined REALones. Add photos and stories that will appear in your "On This Day" feed.
How do I import Facebook friends?
1. Download your friends list from Facebook as a JSON file
2. In REALones, go to Friends → Import Facebook Friends
3. Upload the JSON file
4. Swipe right to keep friends, left to archive
My app is crashing. What should I do?
Try these steps:
  1. Force quit the app and restart
  2. Update to the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the problem persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs
