Frequently Asked Questions

What is REALones?

REALones is a social app designed for your actual friends — not followers. We limit your circle to 200 people max, showing posts in chronological order with no algorithm or ads. It's social media the way it was meant to be.

Why is there a friend limit?

Research shows that humans can only maintain meaningful relationships with about 150-200 people (Dunbar's number). By limiting your circle, we encourage you to focus on the people who actually matter, not accumulating followers.

What are the different friend tiers?

Ride or Dies (4): Your absolute closest people who can message your Besties group.
Squad (12): Your inner circle who can message your Squad group.
Real Ones (200): All your real friends who see your posts.

Is REALones free?

Yes! The free tier includes 100 active friends and 100 archived ("Remember Me") friends. If you need more, premium plans are available starting at $1/month per 10 additional friends.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the Profile tab in the app, or by contacting us at support@real-ones.app. Account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data from our servers.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can request a copy of all your data by contacting support. We'll provide you with an export of your posts, photos, and profile information.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at support@real-ones.app with a description of the issue, your device type, and steps to reproduce the bug. Screenshots are always helpful!

Account Deletion

To delete your REALones account and all associated data:

  1. Open the REALones app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Alternatively, email support@real-ones.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we'll process your request within 48 hours.
